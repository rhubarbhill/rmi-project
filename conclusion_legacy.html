Conclusion: His Legacy

Dr. J. Marion Sims' legacy is complex and controversial, colored by his groundbreaking contributions to gynecology but tainted by his exploitation of enslaved women, bringing a great ethical cost to a scientific breakthrough. It is hard to reconcile scientific progress with our moral responsibility to the American people, but at the very least, we can remember Anarcha, Betsey, and Lucy, the women who were instrumental to Sims' discoveries.
